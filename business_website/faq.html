Frequently Asked Questions

What is ServiceOps Toolkit?

ServiceOps Toolkit is a bundle of Notion and Google Sheets templates, calculators and automation scripts designed specifically for home service providers like handymen, contractors, cleaners and landscapers. It helps you track jobs, manage clients, generate estimates and stay organized without expensive software.

Is this a monthly subscription?

No. Each tier is a one‑time purchase with lifetime access and updates. We believe in helping small businesses without locking you into ongoing fees.

Do I need to know Notion or Google Sheets?

Basic familiarity helps, but we include video tutorials and SOPs to get you up and running quickly. If you can edit a spreadsheet and fill out a form, you’ll be comfortable using the Toolkit.

How do I get support?

We provide step‑by‑step guides, email support and an optional paid onboarding session for Agency customers. You can also join our community forum to get help from other users.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your data lives in your own Notion and Google accounts. We never collect or store your client information. You control who has access to your sheets and databases.

Can I customize the templates?

Absolutely! We encourage you to adapt the templates to your processes. Notion and Google Sheets are flexible platforms, so you can add fields, views and formulas as your business grows.
